Who we are

Accelerating science & elevating its stakeholders

We are a digital PR firm that specializes in using online tools to create and execute smart messaging strategies that enhance signal-to-noise and reach target audiences. Our approach is grounded in the scientific method, fueled by data, and catalyzed by thoughtful storytelling. We build brand awareness for scientists, science-driven companies, and their executive teams. The Stellate team is as diverse as the clients we work with—scientists, designers, writers, web developers, and media specialists—working together to ensure your stories are heard, shared, and remembered.
